Meet Vespucci.

We strongly believe that the content of your application (the emotional content of a publication or the price of an item, ...) transforms the way your customers experience your app. We also think that you would not have to perform complex A/B testing experiments to understand how this transformation occurs. Your application probably already exposes your user to different contexts (an e-commerce app already contains dozens of shoes with different prices a delivery app comes with a ton of different delivery times), it is time to capture how this change the way your users reacts. Without complex implementations.
