What's Drives Value Creation ?

Value creation in your product can be approached and expressed in many different ways. If you are building a social network you will probably identify value as the action of sharing a photo or content by your users. If you run an e-commerce or delivery app you'll probably want to understand what drives your users to place an order or add an item to their cart. You know your business better than anyone else, so this template will guide you in describing to Vespucci how you create value with your app. As you tell him more Vespucci will help you identify the drivers of that value, here are some examples of how Vespucci can help you in this exploration:

How socio-demographic context drives value ?

The elements displayed on the screen are not the only things that can influence the behavior of your users. More intrinsic characteristics such as their level of engagement or the time of the week or the geographical distribution are also likely to influence their behavior. Indeed it could be that users living in the East of Brussels on a weekend have a different reaction than those living in the North on a weekday.

Mix and match your UX with the context.

Very often, an element displayed on the screen such as the price or the delivery time do not have the same influence on the behavior of your users depending on the context. For example, the price will not have the same impact on a purchase behavior on a Friday night or on a Monday at noon. Vespucci has powerful tools to combine these dimensions.
